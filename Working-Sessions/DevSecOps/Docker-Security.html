Docker Security

Track: DevSecOps

Participants(s): Erez Yalon , Francois Raynaud , Helen Bravo , Johan Peeters , Madhu Akula (remotely) , Ohad Rabinovich , Timo Pagel

When:
Time:
Location:

With the advent of Devops, businesses almost cannot avoid containers. They make deploying servers and services significantly easier and more efficient. One of the most widely used tools for deploying containers is Docker. Even with the incredible technology offered by Docker, you still have to keep a keen eye on security. This session will focus on the common pitfalls with regards to Docker security, discuss the aproaches for securing it and the way forward.
